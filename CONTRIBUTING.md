# Contributing to StackCraft Core

StackCraft Core stays **cloud- and vendor-neutral**. Do not add provider-specific how-tos (Terraform modules, cloud console walkthroughs, etc.). Use **stackcraft-setups** for implementation content.

## Suitable contributions

- Clarifications to tier docs, frameworks, checklists, and blueprints
- Diagrams and cross-links where they reduce ambiguity
- Pointers to **stackcraft-sectors** when industry context changes the minimum bar

## Out of scope

- Legal or compliance sign-off; this repository does not certify adherence to any framework
- Marketing or social copy (keep those outside this repository)

## Scope reminder

Content here describes **engineering practices** only. Regulated organizations must involve qualified legal and compliance professionals.
