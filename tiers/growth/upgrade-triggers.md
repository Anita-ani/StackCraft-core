# Growth Production Upgrade Triggers

Move to **Critical** production when:

- **Downtime** or data loss risk becomes **unacceptable** to contracts or leadership — you need evidenced **RTO/RPO** and DR discipline.
- **Compliance** or **enterprise audit** demands formal controls, retention, and access lifecycle beyond Growth baselines.
- **Global availability** or **multi-region** resilience is **required**, not optional.
- **Traffic** or failure modes are **unpredictable** without stronger isolation, HA, and incident program maturity.
- **Recovery time** must approach **near-zero** for key services (active/active, regional failover, runbook automation at scale).
- Incidents expose **gaps in governance**, on-call sustainability, or **single points of leadership** under pressure.
