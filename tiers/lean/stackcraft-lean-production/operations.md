# Operations

## Minimum operational practices

- Staging before production
- Deployment checklist
- Rollback steps documented
- Alerts routed to active team members
- Backup schedule reviewed

## Team fit

Best for:

- 2 to 5 engineers
- Shared ownership of operations
- Growing release frequency

## Deployment expectation

- Automated deploys preferred
- Production changes should be traceable
- Failed deploys should be reversible
