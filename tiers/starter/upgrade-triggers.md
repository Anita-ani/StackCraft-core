# Starter — upgrade triggers

Move to **Lean** when:

- You need **staging** that mirrors prod.  
- **Two+ engineers** collide on deploys.  
- Customer or **payment-adjacent** flows need stricter change control.  
- Alert noise or missing ownership causes repeated fire drills.  
- **PRI** rises.
